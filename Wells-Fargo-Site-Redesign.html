Wells Fargo Site Redesign
Wells Fargo has worked with Staples Promotional Products for a few years now. As their new Art Director in 2024, I was asked to refresh the site look and give more prominent space for marketing the different divisions within Wells Fargo. The result is a look and feel that is more concise with information that’s easier to find. I incorporated some of the newer functionality that is also being used on the United Airlines sites for a fresh and modern user experience.
